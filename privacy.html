Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2, 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you sign in with Google, we receive and store the following information from your Google account:

  • Your name and email address
  • Your profile photo URL
  • A unique Google account identifier

Additionally, we store data you provide while using Treelink:

  • Your chosen username and bio
  • Links you add (titles, URLs, icons)
  • Your theme/accent color preference
  • Click counts on your links (anonymous, aggregate only)

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Create and maintain your Treelink profile
  • Display your public profile page to visitors
  • Provide link analytics (click counts)
  • Authenticate you securely via Google Sign-In

3. Data Storage & Security

All data is stored securely on Google Firebase (Firestore database). Firebase provides enterprise-grade security, encryption in transit and at rest, and regular security audits. We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties.

4. Cookies & Local Storage

Treelink uses cookies only as required by Firebase Authentication to maintain your login session. We do not use tracking cookies or third-party advertising cookies.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Firebase — authentication, database, and hosting
  • Google Fonts — web typography

These services have their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review them.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access, update, or delete your personal data at any time through your dashboard
  • Delete your account by contacting us
  • Request a copy of your stored data

7. Children's Privacy

Treelink is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

9. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please reach out via our homepage.
